
# WellPay — One Page Solution Brief

## Problem

Millions of informal workers in Nigeria face a simple but devastating reality:  
getting sick often means financial strain.

Healthcare is largely paid out-of-pocket, while traditional insurance is:
- expensive
- poorly understood
- difficult to access

As a result, people delay treatment, rely on informal borrowing, or fall into financial distress during health emergencies.

---

## Users

**Primary Users:**
- Market traders
- Artisans
- Transport workers
- Other informal sector earners

**Characteristics:**
- Irregular income patterns  
- Limited access to formal financial services  
- Low insurance adoption  
- High exposure to health-related financial shocks  

---

## Solution (The Build)

WellPay is a mobile-first health financing platform that combines:

- **Health Wallet** — Users save small amounts for medical expenses  
- **Micro-Insurance** — Affordable coverage activated progressively  
- **Health Credit** — Instant funds for urgent health needs  
- **Clinic Access** — Discover and connect with verified providers  
- **Learn & Earn (Optional)** — Bite-sized education that rewards users and improves financial and health literacy  

---

## What Makes It Different

WellPay integrates:

**Savings + Insurance + Credit**

into a single, flexible system.

This allows users to:
- handle immediate health needs (credit)  
- build long-term protection (insurance)  
- develop financial resilience (savings)  

---

## Impact

**For Users:**
- Faster access to healthcare  
- Reduced financial shocks  
- Improved financial and health literacy  
- Increased consistency in saving behavior  

**For the Ecosystem:**
- Higher insurance adoption  
- Better healthcare utilization  
- Reduced reliance on high-risk informal borrowing
- Helps toward achieving of UN SDG goal 3.8: Universal Heath coverage by 2030 

---

## Business Model

- Margin on health credit disbursements  
- Commission from insurance partners  
- Transaction fees on payments  
- Future: embedded services (pharmacy, diagnostics, telemedicine)  

---

## Scalability

WellPay is designed for markets with:
- large informal economies  
- low insurance penetration  
- high mobile phone adoption  

Expansion pathways:
- Telco partnerships  
- Agent networks  
- USSD integration for offline access  

---

## Closing

WellPay turns small, consistent contributions into reliable healthcare access —  
ensuring that getting sick no longer means going broke.
